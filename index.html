Privacy Policy

Mental Math: Focus Trainer respects your privacy.

We use third-party analytics services to understand how users interact with the app and to improve functionality and performance.

This app uses Firebase Analytics, which may collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • App interactions (e.g. screens viewed, actions taken)

This data is used solely for analytics and app improvement purposes. We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.

Analytics data may be processed by Google Firebase in accordance with their privacy policy.
